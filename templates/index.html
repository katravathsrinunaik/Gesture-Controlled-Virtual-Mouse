"Enhancing User Experience: The Gesture-Controlled Virtual Mouse Approach"

To revolutionize computer interaction by implementing a gesture-controlled virtual mouse, offering users a more intuitive and immersive way to navigate digital interfaces.

  • The system utilizes a combination of hardware and software components to track and interpret hand movements, translating them into actions such as cursor movement, clicking, and scrolling.
  • Improved Collaboration: In collaborative IT environments, the gesture-controlled virtual mouse can facilitate easier sharing of screens and information during meetings or troubleshooting sessions, enhancing collaboration and productivity.
  • Innovation and Adaptability: By embracing innovative HCI solutions like the gesture-controlled virtual mouse, the IT sector can demonstrate its commitment to adopting cutting-edge technologies and improving user experiences.
